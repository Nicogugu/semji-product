# Semji — Product Context

## What is Semji

SaaS platform for SEO & content performance, augmented by professional services.
Target: 400+ mid-market & enterprise orgs (250+ employees), primarily in France + North America.

**Core value prop**: Scale organic and generative visibility, increase content productivity, prove ROI, simplify the stack, stay on-brand.

---

## Platform Architecture (5 hubs)

### 1. Content Hub — Create, optimize, publish
- **Content Score** : Score SEO/qualite temps reel avec fixes precis (couverture, profondeur, structure, liens)
- **Brief AI** : Briefs data-driven (keywords, subtopics, intent, questions) en minutes
- **AI+ Content** : Titres, intros, paragraphes, metadata, drafts complets alignes brief + Brand Voice
- **Atomic Content** : Generation de familles de pages SEO a l'echelle (categories, locations, produits)
- **AI Fact-Checking** : Detection/correction inexactitudes, ajout/verification sources
- **AI Brand Voice / Custom Instructions** : Guardrails ton, claims, compliance
- **Content Ideas** : Suggestions keywords et topics pour creer ou optimiser strategiquement
- **SERP & Competitor Analysis** : Gap analysis vs best-in-SERP
- **Incoming Links & Outgoing Links** : Linking contextuel pour renforcer architecture et pertinence
- **Rank Tracking & Reports** : Positions, trends, deltas competitifs lies aux KPIs
- **Collaboration & Comments** : Roles, etapes de review, versioning, notes d'approbation
- **Planning & Editorial Calendar** : Capacity planning, assignments, deadlines
- **Chrome Extension** : Recherche et optimisation partout

### 2. Intelligence Hub — Analyze, prioritize, benchmark
- **Opportunity Discovery** : Quantifie l'upside topic/page (traffic, difficulte, business fit)
- **Competitor Benchmarking** : Visibility share vs peers en SEO + GEO
- **Content Gap & Coverage Maps** : Clusters manquants, gaps de profondeur
- **Performance Forensics** : Explique gains/drops (algo shifts, cannibalisation, concurrents)

### 3. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) — Visibility in AI answers
- **LLM-Answer Readiness** : Optimise le contenu pour etre selectionne/cite dans les reponses IA
- **Citation & Evidence Signals** : Renforce E-E-A-T (sources, authorship, freshness)
- **Prompt-shaped Structuring** : Aligne sections, FAQs, summaries aux patterns IA
- **Monitoring** : Suivi visibilite generative + SEO consolide

### 4. AI Agents — Automate with guardrails
- **Compliance Agent** : Flags claims risques, disclosures manquantes, formulations hors-marque
- **Repurpose Agent** : Convertit long-form en variantes (FAQ, social, email)
- **Internal Linking Agent** : Propose liens haute valeur avec ancres/placements
- **Briefing Agent** : Auto-genere/met a jour les briefs quand les SERPs evoluent
- **Refresh Agent** : Detecte le decay, prepare des updates ciblees

### 5. Foundation — Enterprise-grade infrastructure
- **User & Rights Management** : Roles granulaires, SSO, audit trails
- **Security & Compliance** : SOC 2 Type II, GDPR
- **Reporting Integrations** : Analytics/CRM pour attribuer revenu et pipeline
- **Production Measurements** : Suivi impact business des optimisations
